Global ecommerce sales are forecast to hit $6.09 trillion in 2024 , yet most online stores still present products like digital catalogs from the 1990s. Generic homepage grids display the same featured items to every visitor. Product recommendations follow basic "customers also bought" logic without considering individual preferences. Category pages show identical layouts whether someone prefers contemporary minimalism or traditional comfort.
